Story

Konnichiwa! Every so often a legend is discovered. A noble man at birth with a warrior heart. Fierce is his determination to right those who have been wronged and earn himself a place on the charts of heroes. I am Hattori Hanzo and I have been lost at sea for many moons. Fate has returned me to these lands, for within my mind is inscribed the map of treasures of the secret islands.

I am the greatest of Shinobis and I've battled many a demon and monster both by land and ocean. My title "Oni no Hanzo" was given as there is no greater Demon Shinobi than I. I was born a fighter and transcended into a warrior, protector and strategist. Have you not heard of the legend of how I saved the Leyasu of the Tokugawa clan and helped him become A Ruler?

I have appointed myself your Protector and vow to save you from the other "Oni" roaming the earth. Those who want to control, enslave, and steal from you.

Word has it that you need a leader and I need an army, a match made of circumstance. Stand with me and I will lead you to the most ancient vaults buried deep in the mountains of the Isle of Iga.

We'll need to keep our wits about us, for there are other armies interested in this hidden treasure. The path to Glory shall undoubtedly be riddled with battle upon battle. But fear not for my blade is worth a thousand swords. Together we will strike each and every enemy down and invoke the wrath of the Demon on them!

All I ask is that you remain steadfast for we are only as strong as we are united and weak as we are divided. Remember 1 soldier can win a battle, but it takes an entire army to win the war! So assemble your battle gear and brace yourselves as we step out into the night to pursue that flighty temptress called ADVENTURE!
